About us

school info

Msolwa School was established in January 1994 with a Form One group. It is built by the great local River Iyovi and bordered by large ranges of mountains all round. The school has been performing much better year after year. Our vision as a school is to see a Tanzanian youth with integral personality, able to be good leader, possessing high quality of education and able to pursue high learning.
